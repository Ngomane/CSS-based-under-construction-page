Youth for Change is a youth engagement movement that strives to create a desired future.


Youth capacity-building & technical assistance is what we strive for. We believe that supporting youth-led activities and initiatives ensures the sustainable and significant growth of young people, but only if done in innovative, compelling and relatable ways.


Youth for change is a agent of change via unifying and educating young South Africans aged 12 to 35 on matters that affect us as young people from all walks of life.
